The Bardo Experiment

OVERVIEW

For the Bardo Experiment we had to take the concept of "The Emptiness of the Atom" and create a comic styled story telling game/experience. We had to contemplate the literal science and philosophy of this concept, which lead to some complex and in depth conversations leading us to interesting ideas and various idea potentials. It was fun to working with difficult and complex logic/ideologies, then translating it into a new format.

Brandon Williams · Bardo Experiment OST

During this project there was lull in tasks needing my attention as many on mine were coding tasks set for the later end of the project. Another member made the base coding for several levels, I was to act like a stitcher and new systems coder, up until then my art pieces were largely done and I was on standby. This eventually lead to some strong feelings of imposter syndrome, which my team assured me there was no issues with my work. So with some freetime I looked into some Digital Audio Workstation's (DAW), landing on one called Cakewalk. In my freetime I started playing with Cakewalk and overtime, unprompted, created a soundtrack for The Bardo Experiment.

Please bare in mind that this was all down over the course of a month in a half starting with no experience. Almost exclusively done by ear, I add this to this page for a couple reasons. 1st, it ended up having a huge impact on the vibes and feeling of the scenes. 2nd, as a tool to represent initiative and desire to learn new tools and technology that can assist or improve a projects end goal or potential.
